Our players in this sample game will be known as T-800, Yoda, and Verbal Kint. Verbal shuffles the deck and asks the T-800 Terminator unit to cut the deck. The leather clad, muscle stacked cyborg lifts a giant Bowie knife out of his coat.

"Wait! Stop! Put the knife away. Yoda, you cut the deck."

"But, you towld me to caht da deck. I do naht undahrstand," the Terminator insists.
