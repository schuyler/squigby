Welcome to Squigby!

Welcome to the official homepage of that crazy game of skill at cards, Squigby! Here, you can read the collected rules, go through a less verbose but more understandable tutorial, as well as a handy, step-by-step sample game.

New here? Please e-mail us to let us know what you think. This new game is sweeping the nation, and boy do those bristles sting!

  • Step 1: Read the Squigby! Tutorial
  • Step 2: Follow the Sample Game

For those of you like your Squigby! undiluted, you can read The Grand Collected Rules Of Squigby!, which includes all of the rules described in the Tutorial, plus some more advanced ones!
